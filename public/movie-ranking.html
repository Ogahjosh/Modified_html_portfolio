The Best Movies According to Joshua

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Everybody Hates Chris

This is a very interesting tv series of 4 seasons. I love the characters, the setting and the humour used.

The Adventures of Merlin

This is one of my best tv series ever. It has a mix of comedy, drama romance, suspence and fantasy. I recommend this series to anyone that's looking for something interesting.

Friends

This is one of my favourite comedy shows about a couple of friends and how they go through everyday life. It's funny, dramatic and interesting.
